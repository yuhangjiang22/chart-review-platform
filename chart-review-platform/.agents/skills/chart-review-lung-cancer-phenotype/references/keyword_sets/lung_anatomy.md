# Keyword Set: lung_anatomy

Anatomical vocabulary for identifying lung-related content in clinical notes,
imaging reports, and pathology reports. Used by the criteria `pathology_lung_primary`
and `imaging_lung_lesion` when scanning documents for relevant text.

## Terms

- lung
- lungs
- pulmonary
- parenchyma
- bronchus
- bronchial
- bronchi
- lobe
- lobar
- RUL
- RML
- RLL
- LUL
- LLL

## Synonyms and expansions

| Abbreviation / term | Full form |
|---|---|
| pulmonary | lung |
| RUL | right upper lobe |
| RML | right middle lobe |
| RLL | right lower lobe |
| LUL | left upper lobe |
| LLL | left lower lobe |

## Usage notes

These terms are used as an initial scan filter, not as sufficient evidence on
their own. A document mentioning "pulmonary" or "RUL" requires further review
to determine whether it is describing a malignancy versus a benign or
incidental finding.
