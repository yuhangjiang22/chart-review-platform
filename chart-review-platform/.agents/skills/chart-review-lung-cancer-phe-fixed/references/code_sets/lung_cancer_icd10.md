# Code Set: lung_cancer_icd10

## Lung Cancer Diagnoses (Primary)

- C34.0 — Malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of unspecified lung
- C34.1 — Malignant neoplasm of upper lobe, unspecified lung
- C34.11 — Malignant neoplasm of upper lobe, right lung
- C34.12 — Malignant neoplasm of upper lobe, left lung
- C34.2 — Malignant neoplasm of middle lobe, unspecified lung
- C34.30 — Unspecified malignant neoplasm of lower lobe of unspecified lung
- C34.31 — Unspecified malignant neoplasm of lower lobe of right lung
- C34.32 — Unspecified malignant neoplasm of lower lobe of left lung
- C34.80 — Other malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of unspecified lung
- C34.81 — Other malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of right lung
- C34.82 — Other malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of left lung
- C34.90 — Unspecified malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of unspecified lung
- C34.91 — Unspecified malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of right lung
- C34.92 — Unspecified malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of left lung

## Includes Pattern

- `C34.*` — Captures all malignant neoplasm of lung codes

## Excludes

- Z85.118 — Personal history of malignant neoplasm of unspecified part of unspecified lung (history only, not current diagnosis)
- D38.1 — Neoplasm of uncertain behavior of larynx, trachea and bronchus (not confirmed malignancy)

## Common Histologic Subtypes (when specified)

- C34.* with fifth character for histology when available (8020 = adenocarcinoma, 8070 = squamous cell, 8041 = small-cell)
