MICROSCOPIC DESCRIPTION:
Sections reveal a highly cellular neoplasm composed of small, poorly differentiated cells arranged in sheets and ribbons with focal rosette formation. Individual cells display scant cytoplasm, markedly hyperchromatic nuclei, and finely granular "salt-and-pepper" chromatin. Nuclear molding between adjacent cells is conspicuous. Nucleoli are absent or inconspicuous. Mitotic activity is brisk; mitotic index >25 per 10 HPF. Apoptotic bodies scattered throughout. Large zones of coagulative necrosis present. Crush artifact accounts for approximately 30% of submitted tissue, consistent with fragile small-cell morphology.
